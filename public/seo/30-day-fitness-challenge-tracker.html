Why Most 30-Day Challenges Fail

They're too ambitious. Day 1 enthusiasm sets a pace that Day 12 can't maintain. Momentum solves this by letting you define "showing up" — even a 10-minute walk counts as a check-in.

The goal isn't 30 perfect days. It's 30 days of showing up in some way.
