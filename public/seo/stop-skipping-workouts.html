Skipping workouts is a systems problem, not a character flaw. Without a visible streak to protect, an accountability layer, or a low-friction way to log activity, the default action is always "skip."

Momentum changes the default by making showing up easier than skipping.

3. "I fell off and can't get back." Streak Repair lets you acknowledge the gap and restart without losing progress.
